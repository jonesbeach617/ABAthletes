About Us

ABAthletes combines proven ABA therapy with sports and movement to empower children with special needs. We improve focus, communication, and social skills using active, engaging methods.

Programs We Offer

  • Mini Movers (Ages 3–6): Foundational motor and behavior development
  • TeamUp ABA (Ages 6–10): Group activities focused on teamwork and self-regulation
  • FocusFit (Ages 10–14): Behavior goals + fitness training
